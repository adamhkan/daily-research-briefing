Executive Summary

  • Whole-body and dexterous manipulation see strong advances, from robust loco-manipulation on legged and humanoid robots to language-grounded, functional dexterous grasping.
  • New sensing and sim-to-real pipelines target manipulation reliability and data efficiency, including acoustic slip estimation and physics-aligned simulation for deformables.
  • Mobile manipulation and motion planning safety are emphasized via stability-aware mobile manipulation, competence-aware whole-body tracking, and safe trajectory planning in dynamic unknown environments.
  • Human- and video-centric data sources are leveraged more deeply, with egocentric datasets, smart-glasses teleoperation, and video-generative value models for RL.
  • Control and planning at scale are enabled by GPU-accelerated robust nonlinear MPC and planner-guided RL under partial observability.
  • Institution-aligned work explores low-cost in-hand slip sensing and efficient LLM-based motion planning for autonomous driving via on-policy distillation.

A-SLIP: Acoustic Sensing for Continuous In-hand Slip EstimationCarnegie Mellon UniversityIntroduces A-SLIP, a multi-channel acoustic slip sensing system integrated into a parallel-jaw gripper that infers slip presence, direction, and magnitude from contact-induced vibrations. A lightweight CNN on multi-microphone audio significantly improves slip detection accuracy and directional estimation, enabling reliable, low-cost, real-time feedback for in-hand manipulation control.Link
Karma Mechanisms for Decentralised, Cooperative Multi Agent Path FindingETH ZurichProposes a decentralized coordination framework for multi-agent path finding based on non-tradeable 'karma' credits that encode agents’ past cooperation. By using bilateral negotiations and karma-regulated conflict resolution, the method balances replanning effort and service times across agents while preserving overall efficiency in warehouse-style scenarios.Link
On-Policy Distillation of Language Models for Autonomous Vehicle Motion PlanningStanford University; UC San DiegoBuilds on GPT-Driver to distill a large LLM-based driving planner into a 5× smaller model using on-policy generalized knowledge distillation. The student is trained on its own rollouts with dense token-level feedback from the teacher, achieving near-teacher performance on nuScenes and outperforming a dense-feedback RL baseline for resource-constrained deployment.Link

Sumo: Dynamic and Generalizable Whole-Body Loco-ManipulationPresents a sim-to-real framework enabling legged and humanoid robots to perform dynamic whole-body loco-manipulation with heavy, large objects. A pre-trained whole-body policy is steered at test time by a sample-based planner, allowing task- and object-level generalization via cost shaping, with real-world demonstrations on Spot and simulated humanoids.Link
EMMa: End-Effector Stability-Oriented Mobile Manipulation for Tracked Rescue RobotsIntroduces a motion generation framework for tracked mobile manipulators that jointly optimizes base and arm trajectories to prioritize end-effector stability in rescue scenarios. A coordinated path optimization model with compact constraints plus a feedforward–feedback control scheme yields improved task success and smoother end-effector motion in complex terrain.Link
HEX: Humanoid-Aligned Experts for Cross-Embodiment Whole-Body ManipulationProposes HEX, a state-centric framework for controlling full-sized humanoids via a humanoid-aligned universal state representation and a Mixture-of-Experts proprioceptive predictor. By fusing temporal visual-language context with learned whole-body dynamics, HEX achieves state-of-the-art manipulation performance and generalization, especially in fast and long-horizon tasks.Link
Grasp as You Dream: Imitating Functional Grasping from Generated Human DemonstrationsKTH Royal Institute of Technology; TH Royal InstituteGraspDreamer leverages video generative models trained on internet-scale human data to synthesize functional grasp demonstrations, avoiding costly robot data collection. These demonstrations are combined with robot-specific action optimization to yield zero-shot functional grasping with strong data efficiency and generalization across robot hands and downstream manipulation tasks.Link
CMP: Robust Whole-Body Tracking for Loco-Manipulation via Competence Manifold ProjectionIntroduces Competence Manifold Projection to shield learned whole-body loco-manipulation policies from out-of-distribution commands and sensor noise. By estimating a one-step safety manifold in an isomorphic latent space, the method projects infeasible intents back into safe regions, dramatically improving survival rates while preserving tracking performance.Link
BLaDA: Bridging Language to Functional Dexterous Actions within 3DGS Fields. Li are with the SchoolBLaDA is a modular, interpretable pipeline that maps open-vocabulary language instructions to functional dexterous manipulation in 3D Gaussian Splatting scenes. It parses language into structured constraints, localizes functional regions via a novel TriLocation module, and converts them into wrist and finger commands, yielding superior affordance grounding and manipulation success.Link
SIM1: Physics-Aligned Simulator as Zero-Shot Data Scaler in Deformable WorldsProposes SIM1, a real-to-sim-to-real engine that builds metric-consistent digital twins and calibrated deformable dynamics from limited demonstrations, then scales data via diffusion-based trajectory generation. Policies trained purely on this synthetic data achieve near-real-data performance with strong zero-shot and generalization gains in deformable object manipulation.Link
ActiveGlasses: Learning Manipulation with Active Vision from Ego-centric Human DemonstrationActiveGlasses uses a stereo camera on smart glasses to collect egocentric human demonstrations and then mounts the same camera on a robot arm to mimic human active vision. An object-centric point-cloud policy jointly predicts manipulation and head motions, enabling zero-shot transfer and robust performance on occlusion-heavy tasks across multiple robot platforms.Link
ViVa: A Video-Generative Value Model for Robot Reinforcement LearningViVa repurposes a pretrained video generator to jointly predict future proprioception and a scalar value, grounding RL value estimates in anticipated spatiotemporal dynamics. Integrated into the RECAP framework for real-world assembly, it yields more accurate progress signals and better generalization to novel objects than static vision-language value baselines.Link
Safe Large-Scale Robust Nonlinear MPC in Milliseconds via Reachability-Constrained System Level Synthesis on the GPUGPU-SLS is a GPU-parallelized nonlinear MPC framework that co-optimizes nominal trajectories, tracking controllers, and closed-loop reachable sets under uncertainty. Using a custom GPU QP solver within a system level synthesis formulation, it delivers millisecond-scale, safety-certified control for high-dimensional robots such as quadrupeds and humanoids.Link
