Executive Summary

  • Mobile and whole-body manipulation see advances from lightweight VLAs (AnoleVLA), real-time 3D-aware policies (R3DP), and humanoid loco‑manipulation/RL fine‑tuning (REFINE-DP).
  • Dexterous and contact-rich manipulation is a major focus, spanning new grasp synthesis/learning (DGS-Net, GraspADMM), visuo‑tactile policies (TransDex, TacFiLM), and hybrid force/tactile control frameworks (ForceVLA2, M2-ResiPolicy, HapticVLA).
  • Mixture-of-experts and geometry/action-latent abstractions (MoE-ACT, OPFA, KoopmanFlow) improve multi-task, cross-embodiment, and spectrally decoupled control for complex manipulation.
  • Model-based and optimization-centric motion planning progresses via task-oriented MPC (ToMPC), STL-guided variational trajectory optimization (STL-SVPIO), and fast generative MPC alternatives to diffusion (KDP, IMLE, IMLE-MPC).
  • Benchmarks and datasets expand coverage to non-prehensile, dynamic, long-horizon, and collaborative industrial manipulation (DOMINO, RoCo, LDHP, GroundedPlanBench, Dream2Fix).
  • Institutional highlights: CMU, MIT, ETH Zurich, and NYU contribute on dual-arm agricultural mobile manipulation, underwater opti-acoustic fusion, microgravity RPO simulation, transformer LQR controllers, and egocentric HOI world models.

Field trials show >60% harvest success with <100s cycle time, complemented by a teleoperation fail-safe and a hierarchical indoor/outdoor pepper dataset to support coarse-to-fine detection and pose estimation.Link
Path-conditioned Reinforcement Learning-based Local Planning for Long-Range NavigationETH ZurichThis work proposes an RL-based local navigation policy conditioned on global path observations, using path information implicitly without explicit path-following rewards. The policy exploits high-quality paths when available for efficiency yet remains robust when guidance is degraded or absent, improving long-range navigation with uncertain global plans.Link
SmallSatSim: A High-Fidelity Simulation and Training Toolkit for Microgravity Robotic Close Proximity OperationsETH ZurichSmallSatSim is a MuJoCo-based, GPU-accelerated simulation toolkit for microgravity rendezvous and close-proximity operations, including free-flying manipulation and failure-aware control. It provides high-fidelity dynamics, built-in controllers, configurable fault models, and parallelized training support to accelerate research in autonomous agile small-satellite operations.Link
Sonar-MASt3R: Real-Time Opti-Acoustic Fusion in Turbid, Unstructured EnvironmentsMassachusetts Institute of TechnologySonar-MASt3R fuses optical camera data with sonar-based 3D reconstructions using dense MASt3R correspondences to achieve real-time, dense 3D perception in turbid underwater conditions. Experiments with an eye-in-hand setup across a wide turbidity range show substantially improved robustness over prior opti-acoustic fusion approaches for underwater intervention tasks.Link
Transformers As Generalizable Optimal ControllersCarnegie Mellon UniversityA single transformer policy is trained on LQR-generated trajectories from heterogeneous MIMO LTI systems using a shared padded representation, learning to output near-optimal feedback without explicit plant matrices. The controller exhibits small empirical sub-optimality, stabilizes under moderate parameter changes, and benefits from light fine-tuning on unseen systems.Link
Egocentric World Model for Photorealistic Hand-Object Interaction SynthesisNew York UniversityEgoHOI is an egocentric world model that synthesizes photorealistic, contact-consistent hand–object interaction videos driven purely by action signals, without future state conditioning. By distilling 3D geometric and kinematic priors into physics-informed embeddings, it produces physically plausible egocentric rollouts and outperforms strong video-generation baselines on the HOT3D dataset.Link

End-to-End Dexterous Grasp Learning from Single-View Point Clouds via a Multi-Object Scene DatasetHarbin Institute of TechnologyDGS-Net is an end-to-end network that predicts dense dexterous grasp configurations from single-view point clouds in cluttered multi-object scenes. A two-stage data generation pipeline plus explicit modeling of grasp offsets and pre-grasp poses yields a 78.98% real-robot grasp success rate and lower penetration than existing methods.Link
HapticVLA: Contact-Rich Manipulation via Vision-Language-Action Model without Inference-Time Tactile SensingIntelligent Space Robotics Laboratory, Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology, Moscow, Russia.HapticVLA trains a flow-matching action expert with safety-aware tactile rewards and then distills a compact tactile token into a VLA that uses only vision and state at inference. The resulting model enforces force-aware, contact-safe behavior in real manipulation tasks, achieving 86.7% mean success and outperforming even VLAs that use tactile feedback online.Link
ForceVLA2: Unleashing Hybrid Force-Position Control with Force Awareness for Contact-Rich ManipulationForceVLA2 extends VLAs with hybrid force–position control by introducing force-based prompts and a cross-scale MoE action expert that fuses semantic concepts with real-time force signals. On a new 1,000-trajectory dataset over five contact-rich tasks, it outperforms pi0/pi0.5 by up to 48% and mitigates overload and unstable contact failures.Link
Master Micro Residual Correction with Adaptive Tactile Fusion and Force-Mixed Control for Contact-Rich ManipulationNanyang Technological University; Tsinghua UniversityM2-ResiPolicy is a two-timescale master–micro residual controller that combines a diffusion-based high-level policy with a 60 Hz GRU corrector using wrench feedback. An adaptive fusion module balances visual and tactile cues, and a force-mixed PBIC layer regulates contact forces, yielding a 93% damage-free success rate in fragile and precision tasks.Link
Tactile Modality Fusion for Vision-Language-Action ModelsTacFiLM introduces a lightweight post-training finetuning scheme that conditions intermediate visual features on pretrained tactile embeddings via FiLM, avoiding heavy token concatenation. On insertion tasks, augmenting VLAs with TacFiLM consistently improves success, insertion quality, completion time, and force stability on both in- and out-of-distribution evaluations.Link
TransDex: Pre-training Visuo-Tactile Policy with Point Cloud Reconstruction for Dexterous Manipulation of Transparent ObjectsTransDex pre-trains a transformer to reconstruct object point clouds from noisy, masked interaction observations, then uses this representation for a visuo-tactile dexterous control policy. The approach handles depth noise and self-occlusion with transparent objects, outperforming baselines in real-world dexterous manipulation experiments and showing good generalization.Link
GraspADMM: Improving Dexterous Grasp Synthesis via ADMM OptimizationPeking UniversityGraspADMM formulates grasp refinement with ADMM to decouple target contact points on the object from realized contacts on the hand, alternating between optimizing grasp stability metrics and enforcing collision-free reachability. It substantially boosts grasp success (≈15% absolute in type-unaware, ≈100% relative in type-aware settings) while preserving physical plausibility even under low friction.Link
