## Executive Summary

- Dexterous and whole-body manipulation sees multiple advances, from human-in-the-loop VLA correction and history-aware diffusion policies to unified embodied models that jointly learn perception, reasoning, imagination, and action.
- Navigation and motion planning are strengthened by frontier- and uncertainty-aware UAV MPC, safety-constrained RL with reachability verification, and graph-of-convex-sets optimization for autonomous driving.
- New autonomous driving planners leverage flow matching, closed-loop value estimation and ranking, and multi-agent latent rollouts to close the gap between imitation learning and real-world rule-based metrics.
- Safety, monitoring, and robustness are emphasized through vision-based temporal-logic runtime monitors, semantic attacks on HD map construction, and LiDAR odometry unified with semantic occupancy grids.
- Surveys and analysis pieces map out the state of dexterous hand intelligence, quantify the role of 3D perception in VLN, and propose ergodic imitation and temporal priors as key design levers for robust control.
