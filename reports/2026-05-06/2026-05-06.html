Executive Summary

  • Safety and formal methods see strong advances, from online safety filters for deformable manipulation to temporal-logic value functions and reach-avoid tubes for high-dimensional systems.
  • Manipulation capabilities are pushed via visuo-haptic shape completion, visibility-aware mobile grasping, desk-organization primitives, and several VLA-based and diffusion-based visuomotor policies.
  • Whole-body and legged control progresses with proprioception-only wrench estimation, dynamics-aware quadrupedal locomotion, humanoid force-adaptive loco-manipulation, and soft/continuum control.
  • New planning and control formulations span stochastic STL-constrained feedback motion planning, optimal-transport MPC, belief-space trajectory-trees, and multi-agent and UAV planners.
  • Sensing, hardware, and data infrastructure for dexterous and VLA-based systems expand with scalable hand sensing, conformal shape-mapping skins, low-cost manipulation platforms, and phone-based teleoperation.
  • Multiple works focus on long-horizon robustness and metacognition in VLA models, adding anticipation, adaptive test-time compute, and execution monitoring with automatic continual data collection.

Online Safety Filter for Deformable Object Manipulation with Horizon Agnostic Neural OperatorsCarnegie Mellon UniversityIntroduces a real-time safety filter for deformable object manipulation that wraps any nominal controller with explicit task-level safety constraints. A horizon-agnostic neural operator models PDE boundary dynamics, while a control barrier function enforces safety via a small QP, improving safe-fluid-manipulation trajectories over reward-shaped baselines.Link
Robust Adaptive Predictive Control for Hook-Based Aerial Transportation Between Moving PlatformsETH ZurichPresents an MPC framework for hook-equipped quadrotors performing pick-and-place between moving platforms, using a MuJoCo-based digital twin as the prediction model. Zero-order robust optimization combined with EKF-based parameter estimation yields adaptive, constraint-satisfying control validated in complex simulations and real flights.Link
High-Speed, Scalable Sensor Readout for Dexterous Robotic Hands via Shift-Register MultiplexingETH ZurichProposes a serial-in parallel-out shift-register architecture for high-bandwidth, low-wiring readout of heterogeneous analog sensors in dexterous hands. A 20-channel prototype achieves ≥1 kHz full-scan rates with low error, enabling accurate joint and tactile feedback for real-time dexterous manipulation.Link
IMPACT-HOI: Supervisory Control for Onset-Anchored Partial HOI Event ConstructionETH ZurichIntroduces a mixed-initiative system for annotating egocentric videos with structured HOI event graphs, targeting supervision for robot manipulation learning. A trust-calibrated controller chooses between queries, suggestions, and safe completions, reducing manual actions while preserving annotator-confirmed decisions.Link
Neural Backward Reach-Avoid Tubes with MPC Supervision for High-Dimensional Systems: An Application to Safe Spacecraft DockingStanford UniversityDevelops a neural approximation of Hamilton–Jacobi reach-avoid value functions for high-dimensional docking dynamics, supervised both by PDE losses and curriculum MPC targets. The learned value is used for gradient-based control and as a terminal cost in MPC, yielding superior success and efficiency on 6D and 13D docking problems.Link
Dynamics Aware Quadrupedal Locomotion via Intrinsic Dynamics HeadNew York UniversityProposes joint training of a quadruped control policy with an auxiliary Intrinsic Dynamics head that learns state-to-torque mappings and shapes a dynamics-aware reward. This encourages predictable dynamics, improving efficiency and smoothness in simulation and transferring to hardware with notable gains in torque efficiency and safety metrics.Link
Value Functions for Temporal Logic: Optimal Policies and Safety FiltersMassachusetts Institute of Technology; UC San DiegoAnalyzes value and policy optimality for undiscounted infinite-horizon tasks expressed in temporal logic, showing that greedy Q policies can be suboptimal for Until-style specifications. Constructs history-dependent policies on graphs of TL value functions that are provably optimal and demonstrates how Q-functions can act as general TL safety filters.Link

Visibility-Aware Mobile Grasping in Dynamic EnvironmentsPresents a unified system for mobile grasping under limited FOV and dynamic obstacles, coupling a whole-body planner with active perception and a behavior-tree high-level planner. Demonstrated on a Fetch platform and in 400 simulations, it substantially improves success and collision safety in unknown static and dynamic scenes.Link
Feedback Motion Planning for Stochastic Nonlinear Systems with Signal Temporal Logic SpecificationsProvides a feedback motion-planning pipeline for continuous-time stochastic systems under STL with chance constraints, using predicate erosion driven by probabilistic reachable tubes. Contraction-based tracking controllers bound stochastic deviations, enabling deterministic STL trajectory optimization with high satisfaction probabilities on robots including a quadruped.Link
Sampling-Based Control via Entropy-Regularized Optimal TransportIntroduces OT-MPC, which reframes sampling-based MPC as entropy-regularized optimal transport between candidate controls and low-cost proposals, optimized via Sinkhorn iterations. This geometry-aware update avoids mode-averaging pathologies of MPPI/CEM and improves success on navigation, manipulation, and locomotion tasks.Link
Optimizing Trajectory-Trees in Belief Space: An Application from Model Predictive Control to Task and Motion PlanningArgues for belief-space trajectory-trees instead of single trajectories in partially observable settings, enabling branching at informative observations. Proposes a distributed augmented Lagrangian optimizer for tree-shaped MPC and extends Logic-Geometric Programming to partially observable TAMP, with examples in autonomous driving and small belief spaces.Link
Robotic Desk Organization: A Multi-Primitive Approach to Manipulating Heterogeneous Objects via Environmental ConstraintsDevelops a desk-organization framework combining augmented perception with environment-assisted primitives like edge-based push-grasping and levering for deformables. A task planner sequences these primitives to organize mixed rigid and deformable planar objects, with real-world experiments showing robust performance.Link
ShapeGrasp: Simultaneous Visuo-Haptic Shape Completion and Grasping for Improved Robot ManipulationCzech Technical University in PragueIntroduces an iterative pipeline that alternates between visuo-haptic shape completion and simulated grasp planning, updating the object model after each grasp with tactile and occupancy cues. On two robots and grippers, ShapeGrasp improves both 3D reconstruction metrics and real-world grasp success, reaching up to 91% success with a two-finger hand.Link
CoRAL: Contact-Rich Adaptive LLM-based Control for Robotic ManipulationProposes CoRAL, where LLMs synthesize context-aware cost functions for an MPPI controller instead of directly outputting actions, and a VLM-plus-online-ID loop refines physical parameters. A retrieval memory reuses strategies, enabling zero-shot planning and real-time contact-rich control that outperforms VLA and foundation-model planners in challenging scenarios.Link
Decompose and Recompose: Reasoning New Skills from Existing Abilities for Cross-Task Robotic ManipulationPresents a skill-reasoning framework that decomposes demonstrations into atomic skill–action pairs and recomposes them for unseen tasks via compositional reasoning. Dynamic and static demonstration libraries ensure coverage of skill patterns, yielding strong zero-shot cross-task performance on AGNOSTOS and real robots.Link
SixthSense: Task-Agnostic Proprioception-Only Whole-Body Wrench Estimation for HumanoidsHong Kong University of Science and Technology; Tencent Robotics X; Zhejiang UniversityIntroduces SixthSense, which uses conditional flow matching over proprioception and IMU histories to infer sparse whole-body contact events and wrenches without external force sensors. The plug-and-play module improves collision detection, physical HRI, and teleoperation across standing, walking, and whole-body tracking behaviors.Link
VOFA: Visual Object Goal Pushing with Force-Adaptive Control for HumanoidsCombines a goal-conditioned visuomotor policy with a force-adaptive whole-body controller to enable humanoid robots to push heavy objects to visual goals under uncertainty. Evaluated on the Booster T1 humanoid, VOFA achieves >90% success in simulation and >80% on hardware, handling objects up to ~17 kg.Link
