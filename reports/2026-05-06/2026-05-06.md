## Executive Summary

- Safety and formal methods see strong advances, from online safety filters for deformable manipulation to temporal-logic value functions and reach-avoid tubes for high-dimensional systems.
- Manipulation capabilities are pushed via visuo-haptic shape completion, visibility-aware mobile grasping, desk-organization primitives, and several VLA-based and diffusion-based visuomotor policies.
- Whole-body and legged control progresses with proprioception-only wrench estimation, dynamics-aware quadrupedal locomotion, humanoid force-adaptive loco-manipulation, and soft/continuum control.
- New planning and control formulations span stochastic STL-constrained feedback motion planning, optimal-transport MPC, belief-space trajectory-trees, and multi-agent and UAV planners.
- Sensing, hardware, and data infrastructure for dexterous and VLA-based systems expand with scalable hand sensing, conformal shape-mapping skins, low-cost manipulation platforms, and phone-based teleoperation.
- Multiple works focus on long-horizon robustness and metacognition in VLA models, adding anticipation, adaptive test-time compute, and execution monitoring with automatic continual data collection.

| IMPACT-HOI: Supervisory Control for Onset-Anchored Partial HOI Event Construction | ETH Zurich | Introduces a mixed-initiative system for annotating egocentric videos with structured HOI event graphs, targeting supervision for robot manipulation learning. | Neural Backward Reach-Avoid Tubes with MPC Supervision for High-Dimensional Systems: An Application to Safe Spacecraft Docking | Stanford University | Develops a neural approximation of Hamilton–Jacobi reach-avoid value functions for high-dimensional docking dynamics, supervised both by PDE losses and curriculum MPC targets. The learned value is used for gradient-based control and as a terminal cost in MPC, yielding superior success and efficiency on 6D and 13D docking problems. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02021 |
| Dynamics Aware Quadrupedal Locomotion via Intrinsic Dynamics Head | New York University | Proposes joint training of a quadruped control policy with an auxiliary Intrinsic Dynamics head that learns state-to-torque mappings and shapes a dynamics-aware reward. This encourages predictable dynamics, improving efficiency and smoothness in simulation and transferring to hardware with notable gains in torque efficiency and safety metrics. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01227 |
| Value Functions for Temporal Logic: Optimal Policies and Safety Filters | Massachusetts Institute of Technology; UC San Diego | Analyzes value and policy optimality for undiscounted infinite-horizon tasks expressed in temporal logic, showing that greedy Q policies can be suboptimal for Until-style specifications. Constructs history-dependent policies on graphs of TL value functions that are provably optimal and demonstrates how Q-functions can act as general TL safety filters. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01051 |
