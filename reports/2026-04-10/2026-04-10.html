Executive Summary

  • Differentiable and diffusion-based planners push multi-agent navigation and multi-robot path planning toward gradient-based, train-small-deploy-large formulations.
  • Generative and flow-matching motion planners for manipulators capture multi-modal path distributions, improving success and inference-time optimization.
  • New datasets, hardware, and frameworks for bimanual dexterous and tactile-aware manipulation significantly boost performance on contact-rich tasks.
  • Skill adaptation and transfer methods, including residual RL and reachability-aware policy transfer, improve robustness and cross-embodiment reuse of manipulation skills.
  • Rich reachability maps and fast panoramic scene reconstruction tools strengthen the infrastructure for scalable manipulation planning and simulation.
  • Adaptive perception pipelines for embedded platforms close the loop between navigation requirements and depth-estimation compute, improving energy efficiency.

Differentiable Environment-Trajectory Co-Optimization for Safe Multi-Agent NavigationETH ZurichFormulates safe multi-agent navigation as a bi-level optimization over both agent trajectories and environment configuration. Uses KKT conditions and the Implicit Function Theorem to differentiate through an interior-point trajectory optimizer, enabling gradient-based environment design that improves safety and efficiency in scenarios from warehouses to urban traffic.Link
CADENCE: Context-Adaptive Depth Estimation for Navigation and Computational EfficiencyUC San DiegoIntroduces CADENCE, a closed-loop system that adaptively scales a slimmable monocular depth network based on navigation context on embedded platforms. On a Jetson Orin Nano with AirSim, it cuts energy use by 75% and latency by ~75% while slightly improving navigation accuracy by selectively invoking high-fidelity depth only when mission-critical.Link

Flow Motion Policy: Manipulator Motion Planning with Flow Matching ModelsTexas A&M UniversityProposes an end-to-end, open-loop neural motion planner for manipulators based on flow matching, modeling a distribution over feasible paths rather than a single solution. At inference, it samples multiple trajectories and selects the first collision-free one, improving planning success and efficiency over sampling-based and prior neural planners.Link
Train-Small Deploy-Large: Leveraging Diffusion-Based Multi-Robot PlanningUniversity of VirginiaIntroduces a diffusion-model-based multi-robot path planner trained on a small number of agents that generalizes to substantially larger teams at deployment. A shared diffusion backbone with inter-agent attention and temporal convolutions enables scalable, accurate plans compared to multi-agent RL and heuristic baselines.Link
Learning-Based Strategy for Composite Robot Assembly Skill AdaptationPresents a modular composite-skill representation for peg-in-hole assembly combined with residual reinforcement learning for contact-rich refinement. The structure keeps high-level pre/post conditions fixed while RL learns residual corrections per skill, yielding robust, sample-efficient execution on a simulated UR5e.Link
BiDexGrasp: Coordinated Bimanual Dexterous Grasps across Object Geometries and SizesReleases BiDexGrasp, a large-scale bimanual dexterous grasp dataset with 6,351 objects and 9.7M grasps generated via a two-stage synthesis pipeline. Builds a generative model with explicit bimanual coordination and geometry/size adaptation that produces high-quality grasps on unseen objects in both simulation and real hardware.Link
TAMEn: Tactile-Aware Manipulation Engine for Closed-Loop Data Collection in Contact-Rich TasksDevelops a cross-morphology wearable teleoperation interface and dual-mode tracking pipeline (motion capture and VR) for collecting visuo-tactile demonstrations and recovery data in bimanual, contact-rich manipulation. The resulting pyramid-structured data regime and learning framework boost task success from 34% to 75%, and the hardware and dataset are open-sourced.Link
RichMap: A Reachability Map Balancing Precision, Efficiency, and Flexibility for Rich Robot Manipulation TasksIntroduces RichMap, a refined grid-based reachability map on S²/SO(3) that attains >98% prediction accuracy with low false positives and ~15 μs query times. Demonstrates applications including workspace similarity via MMD and energy-based guidance for diffusion policy transfer, improving cross-embodiment block pushing performance by up to 26%.Link
Sustainable Transfer Learning for Adaptive Robot SkillsEmpirically studies transferring RL peg-in-hole policies across different robot platforms under zero-shot, fine-tuning, and from-scratch regimes. Finds that fine-tuning transferred policies substantially improves success and efficiency versus zero-shot and full retraining, underscoring the value of adaptation for sustainable skill reuse.Link
Towards Multi-Object Nonprehensile Transportation via Shared Teleoperation: A Framework Based on Virtual Object Model Predictive ControlProposes a shared teleoperation framework where the human commands tray position while an MPC controller regulates orientation using a virtual-object dynamic model. This approach enforces stability constraints for multiple items on a tray, reducing sliding distance by ~72% and eliminating tip-overs in experiments with up to nine objects.Link
Occlusion Handling by Pushing for Enhanced Fruit DetectionMonash UniversityCombines generative depth completion, image-based push direction estimation, and a 3D Hough transform for branch detection to clear occlusions in orchard environments. A robot arm pushes identified branches to expose fruits, significantly improving visibility and enabling successful real-world demonstrations across multiple fruit types.Link
Genie Sim PanoRecon: Fast Immersive Scene Generation from Single-View PanoramaPresents a feed-forward Gaussian splatting pipeline that converts a single panoramic image into a consistent 3D scene in seconds. By splitting the panorama into cube faces and injecting depth for coherence, it provides photorealistic, low-cost environments integrated into Genie Sim for scalable manipulation data generation and evaluation.Link
