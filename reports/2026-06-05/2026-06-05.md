## Executive Summary

- Humanoid whole-body control and loco-manipulation continue to advance, with new multi-modal controllers and large-scale virtual data pipelines enabling high sim-to-real success on Unitree G1.
- Navigation and motion planning under uncertainty are a major focus, spanning agile quadrotor world models, unified visual navigation with latent imagination, and long-horizon online POMDP solvers.
- Risk-aware and safety-first frameworks emerge across MPC, multi-agent exploration, and safe RL, emphasizing distribution-free guarantees and structured handling of multi-objective uncertainty.
- New benchmarks and datasets target challenging domains such as underground exploration, deformable object and DLO manipulation, and visuotactile, language-conditioned control.
- Semantic and world-model-based representations (occupancy grids, semantic SLAM, action-conditioned video models) are increasingly used to close the loop between perception, planning, and policy evaluation.
- LLM- and VLA-centric tools are being systematically upgraded via better data curation, 3D and focus priors, and simulation-based verification to improve robustness and physical feasibility in manipulation and navigation.
