Executive Summary

  • Motion-planning and control see multiple MPPI advances and control-aware trajectory optimization, improving navigation and manipulator performance in clutter and dynamic environments.
  • New frameworks for dexterous and whole-body manipulation (LAMP, WristMimic, RynnWorld-4D, FORGE, DexTele) emphasize latent action spaces, 4D world models, and tool/function-level generalization.
  • Mobile and embodied navigation is pushed by unified last-mile reasoning (UniLM-Nav), 360° geometry-aware directional reasoning (EAGOR), neural simulation at scale (Image2Sim), and new SLAM/occupancy benchmarks.
  • VLA/world-model ecosystems continue to mature, with structure-aware data selection (SIEVE), training-free/action-aware acceleration and RL post-training (ActionCache, OTQL), and more practical whole-body VLA deployments.
  • New datasets and benchmarks (Hilti-Trimble-Oxford 360 VI-SLAM, OrchardBench, GraspIT, ThorArena, HIOcc, APVI-SLAM) target robust navigation, mapping, grasping, and contact-rich humanoid interaction.
  • Assistive, agricultural, and vehicular applications benefit from autonomous powered wheelchairs, orchard harvesting simulation, and uncertainty-aware proprioceptive localization and PDR filters.

Clustering-Embedded Model Predictive Path Integral Control: Avoiding Averaging-Induced Failure and Enabling Efficient Cluster Selection for Dynamic ObstaclesUniversity of WashingtonIntroduces CE-MPPI, which embeds pruning and DBSCAN-based clustering into MPPI to separate feasible from infeasible trajectory modes in cluttered, non-convex spaces. The method reduces obstacle-front hesitation and improves performance in dynamic scenes, yielding 48% faster time-to-goal and 12% shorter paths on a UR5e.Link
Hilti-Trimble-Oxford Dataset: 360 Visual-Inertial Benchmark with Floor Plan Priors for SLAM and LocalizationETH ZurichReleases a 360° visual–inertial dataset from an active multi-floor construction site, with LiDAR-inertial ground truth and floor-plan priors for SLAM and localization. The associated challenge highlights strong SLAM maturity but higher difficulty for floor-plan-referenced localization, motivating further research for navigation in construction environments.Link

UniLM-Nav: A Unified Framework for Zero-Shot Last-Mile NavigationProposes UniLM-Nav, which uses a shared multimodal LLM backend to select views, ground task-conditioned affordances, and infer manipulation-ready base poses for mobile manipulators. On the OVMM benchmark and a Unitree B2 + Z1 platform, it outperforms prior work for open-vocabulary last-mile navigation.Link
MP-MPPI: A Motion Primitive Guided Sampling-Based Optimizer for Model Predictive ControlNorwegian University of Science and TechnologyExtends MPPI by injecting motion primitives into the sampling process, improving exploration of the control space while retaining real-time responsiveness. Demonstrated on quadcopter obstacle-field navigation, it enhances path-planning capability over standard MPPI.Link
EAGOR: Embodied Reasoning in Omni-directionIntroduces a training-free, geometry-aware framework for 360° directional reasoning, maintaining a spherical belief over target directions using Spherical Harmonic Belief Fields. EAGOR improves map-free navigation and object search success and efficiency for legged robots relative to ERP-based VLM baselines.Link
Image2Sim: Scaling Embodied Navigation via Generative Neural SimulatorPresents a neural simulation pipeline that turns posed RGB-D sequences into interactive scenes using feature Gaussians and a one-step pixel-flow renderer. Image2Sim generates ~20K scenes and 10M samples, enabling navigation policies trained purely in neural simulators to transfer strongly to real-world tasks.Link
Towards Real-World Applications with an Autonomous Powered WheelchairBuilds an autonomous prototype on a commercial self-balancing powered wheelchair by integrating RGB-D perception, LiDAR-based localization, and gesture-based interaction. Demonstrates hailing and people-following behaviors, illustrating challenges and feasibility of assistive navigation in real indoor environments.Link
APVI-SLAM: Real-Time Acoustic-Pressure-Visual-Inertial Localization and Photorealistic Mapping System in Complex Underwater EnvironmentHong Kong University of Science and TechnologyProposes APVI-SLAM, a multi-sensor underwater SLAM system that fuses acoustic, pressure, visual, and inertial data with reliability-aware weighting and a sliding-window freezing strategy. It achieves robust localization and Gaussian-based photorealistic mapping in challenging subsea conditions, and introduces a coral-reef dataset.Link
GEM-Occ: From Visual Geometry Evidence to Embodied Semantic Occupancy MemoryReleases HIOcc, a unified semantic occupancy benchmark across ScanNet, ScanNet++, and Matterport3D, and introduces GEM-Occ, which fuses transient geometric evidence into a hierarchical Gaussian occupancy memory. The approach improves long-horizon indoor semantic mapping, free-space reasoning, and revisit consistency for navigation agents.Link
Why does Deep Learning Improve Visual SLAM?University of ZurichPerforms a controlled study dissecting deep visual SLAM components, showing that learned 2D data association and uncertainty—not recurrence alone—drive performance gains under difficult visual conditions. The results argue for learning-based association and uncertainty modeling as key levers for robust navigation SLAM.Link
Quaternion-Averaging-Based Adaptive Complementary Filter for Pedestrian Dead Reckoning With a Foot-Mounted AHRSKA and its promotion funds from KEIRIN RACE.The authors are with Graduate SchoolIntroduces QAACF, which fuses angular-velocity-derived quaternions with acceleration and magnetometer quaternions via Markley’s quaternion averaging for a foot-mounted AHRS. By adapting sensor weights to gait phase and magnetic disturbance, it improves attitude and hence PDR accuracy at lower computational cost than Kalman filters.Link
Uncertainty-Aware Velocity Correction for Proprioceptive Vehicle Localization using Evidential MambaTechnical University of MunichProposes EVC-Mamba, a Mamba-based temporal model that turns onboard vehicle sensor streams into uncertainty-aware virtual velocity measurements fused in an error-state EKF. It reduces inertial drift during GNSS outages, approaching the performance of dedicated external velocity sensors while running at 40 Hz on edge hardware.Link
