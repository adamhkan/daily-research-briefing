## Executive Summary

- Motion-planning and control see multiple MPPI advances and control-aware trajectory optimization, improving navigation and manipulator performance in clutter and dynamic environments.
- New frameworks for dexterous and whole-body manipulation (LAMP, WristMimic, RynnWorld-4D, FORGE, DexTele) emphasize latent action spaces, 4D world models, and tool/function-level generalization.
- Mobile and embodied navigation is pushed by unified last-mile reasoning (UniLM-Nav), 360° geometry-aware directional reasoning (EAGOR), neural simulation at scale (Image2Sim), and new SLAM/occupancy benchmarks.
- VLA/world-model ecosystems continue to mature, with structure-aware data selection (SIEVE), training-free/action-aware acceleration and RL post-training (ActionCache, OTQL), and more practical whole-body VLA deployments.
- New datasets and benchmarks (Hilti-Trimble-Oxford 360 VI-SLAM, OrchardBench, GraspIT, ThorArena, HIOcc, APVI-SLAM) target robust navigation, mapping, grasping, and contact-rich humanoid interaction.
- Assistive, agricultural, and vehicular applications benefit from autonomous powered wheelchairs, orchard harvesting simulation, and uncertainty-aware proprioceptive localization and PDR filters.
