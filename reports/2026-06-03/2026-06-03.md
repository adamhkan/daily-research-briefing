## Executive Summary

- Dexterous and contact-rich manipulation saw advances in diffusion-based grasp generation (NDPP-Grasp, GraspGen-X), affordance and action manifolds (AFUN, LDA, GAM), and passivity-shielded VLA control (PaCo-VLA).
- Mobile and aerial navigation work emphasized semantic reasoning and uncertainty handling, from probabilistic scene graphs and semantic ESDFs to UAV VLN with kinodynamic planning and expert-guided RL.
- Humanoid and whole-body control progressed via perceptive locomotion encoders (GLAD), constrained whole-body tracking (ConstrainedMimic), universal action spaces (PHASOR), and loco-manipulation in photorealistic sim (LEGS, HOIST).
- Vision-Language-Action models are being unified and hardened: OneVLA and Continuous Reasoning target general-purpose control, while MPVI, PACE, CaB, PaCo-VLA, and AHEAD improve robustness, timing, and safety without retraining.
- Learned world models and generative planners (τ₀-WM, RoboDream, SKIP, CoFi, StressDream, DriveAnchor) are maturing into infrastructure for planning, data synthesis, and stress-testing across manipulation and autonomous driving.
- Multi-robot and swarm systems gained new theory and tools, from diffusion-guided multi-robot motion planning and kinetic information transport models to adaptive CBF-based reconnection and physics-informed swarm controllers.
