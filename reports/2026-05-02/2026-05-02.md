## Executive Summary

- Strong focus on contact-rich manipulation and task/motion planning, with new global trajectory optimization (Global-MPPI) and active-perception TAMP (VAP-TAMP).
- Quadrupedal loco-manipulation advances toward whole-body mobile manipulation by integrating visuo-tactile hierarchy and large-scale RL with zero-shot sim-to-real transfer.
- Foundation VLA and world models (LaST-R1, PRTS, MotuBrain, ExoActor) target long-horizon, instruction-following manipulation and humanoid control with improved dynamics and goal-reachability awareness.
- Dexterous manipulation capabilities are pushed by function-based robotic hand co-design and improved modeling of redundant parallel manipulators’ internal forces.
- New dynamics and simulation tools (RopeDreamer for deformable objects, DOT-Sim for optical tactile sensors) support more realistic planning and control in complex contact scenarios.
- Navigation under dense dynamic obstacles benefits from hybrid latent-dynamics + MPPI methods that blend learned long-horizon intent with short-horizon physics-based safety.
