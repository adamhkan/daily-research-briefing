## Executive Summary

- Strong push on high-speed, reliable motion generation: SIMD-accelerated multi-arm planning (VAMP-MR) and robust LiDAR/SLAM/localization pipelines for demanding environments.
- Mobile and open-world manipulation see new data and agentic frameworks, from synthetic “one-demo” engines (WANDA) to fully embodied, interactive systems (REAL).
- Navigation work spans language-driven goal setting, zero-shot object-goal exploration, risk-aware mapping, and unified decision-making/trajectory planning for complex traffic and UAV scenarios.
- Manipulation research emphasizes data efficiency and robustness: reversed easy-task supervision, one-shot constrained skills, deformable-object models, and robust execution/representation learning for VLA policies.
- Safety, uncertainty, and human interaction are recurrent themes: risk-layered cost maps, barrier-function IRL, expressive manipulator motions for communicating uncertainty, and scene-graph/memory systems to combat perceptual saturation.
- Benchmarks and surveys (industrial dexterity benchmark, UAV coverage review, electrostatic haptics survey) signal maturing subfields and clearer problem definitions in dexterous manipulation, coverage planning, and haptic actuation.
