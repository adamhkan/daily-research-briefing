## Executive Summary

- New whole-body control methods advance humanoid fighting, non-self-stabilizing motions, and dual-mode wheel-legged loco-manipulation.
- FingerViP demonstrates high-success real-world dexterous manipulation via fingertip cameras and diffusion-based visuomotor policies.
- Trace-conditioned and view-robust VLA frameworks significantly improve long-horizon and cross-view manipulation robustness.
- Safety and planning advances include full-body CBF-based collision avoidance and waypoint-based bi-level multi-robot planning.
- ETH Zurich presents a compact, magneto-elastic peristaltic pump, while CMU demonstrates Bayesian reasoning for autonomous casualty triage.
- World-model-based post-training and new evaluation protocols clarify how VLAs behave and fail in open-world environments.
