## 1) Executive Summary

- Strong cluster on **manipulation and mobile manipulation**, including bimanual dexterous grasping, large-workspace humanoid manipulation with omnidirectional perception, long-horizon diffusion policies, and sim-to-real tactile dexterity.
- Several works advance **motion planning and whole-body control**: GPU-accelerated dynamics-aware planning for high-DoF manipulators/humanoids, transformer-based informed sampling, Riemannian-manifold dynamical systems, and robust joint-space planning over multiple IK goals.
- Multiple papers target **risk, safety, and uncertainty in navigation and manipulation**, via evidential waypoint prediction, risk-aware RL for mobile manipulation, distributionally robust collision avoidance, and semantics-aware safety fields.
- Emerging emphasis on **memory and hierarchy for VLA/VLM-based control**, including interpretability and feature steering in VLAs, memory benchmarks and architectures, tri-system VLM–VLA–critic loops, and offline latent policy steering.
- Foundational infrastructure contributions span **SLAM, digital twins, and tools**, including dense monocular SLAM with foundation models, Gaussian-splatting-based digital twins for manipulation, Python bindings for large motion-planning libraries, and overhead-LiDAR person tracking in industrial settings.
