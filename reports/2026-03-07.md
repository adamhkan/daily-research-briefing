| cuRoboV2: Dynamics-Aware Motion Generation with Depth-Fused Distance Fields for High-DoF Robots | NVIDIA | Presents a unified GPU-native motion generation stack combining B-spline trajectory optimization with torque limits, a TSDF/ESDF pipeline producing dense distance fields up to 10× faster and 8× more memory-efficient, and scalable whole-body kinematics/dynamics for high-DoF humanoids. Demonstrates near-100% success in manipulation and humanoid IK plus improved locomotion policies, effectively bridging fast planning, perception, and dynamics feasibility. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05493 |
| GAIDE: Graph-based Attention Masking for Spatial- and Embodiment-aware Motion Planning | Texas A&M University | Proposes a transformer-based neural informed sampler that encodes robot embodiment and workspace structure as a graph, injecting this via attention masking to bias sampling for sampling-based planners. Experiments show improved success and efficiency over uniform, hand-crafted, and prior neural samplers (MPNet, SIMPNet) in high-DoF manipulator planning. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04463 |
| U-OBCA: Uncertainty-Aware Optimization-Based Collision Avoidance via Wasserstein Distributionally Robust Chance Constraints | Shanghai Jiao Tong University | Extends Optimization-Based Collision Avoidance to polygonal robots/obstacles under uncertainty, formulating distributionally robust chance constraints over relative pose. Derives deterministic nonlinear constraints under mild assumptions, solving them efficiently while greatly reducing conservatism versus circle/ellipse approximations, particularly in narrow environments. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04914 |
| Many-RRT*: Robust Joint-Space Trajectory Planning for Serial Manipulators | USACE ERDC | Introduces an RRT*-Connect variant that plans to multiple IK goal configurations in parallel, treating multiple joint-space preimages as a multi-armed bandit. By growing trees from many candidate goal states, it improves success rate and reduces path cost in cluttered environments where single-goal RRT variants often fail. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04547 |
| Omni-Manip: Beyond-FOV Large-Workspace Humanoid Manipulation with Omnidirectional 3D Perception | HKUST (Guangzhou) & Tsinghua University | Trains an end-to-end LiDAR-driven visuomotor policy for humanoid manipulation over large workspaces using panoramic 3D point clouds and a Time-Aware Attention Pooling module. Enables whole-body humanoid manipulation without frequent base repositioning, outperforming egocentric RGB-D baselines in cluttered and beyond-field-of-view tasks. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05355 |
| PhysiFlow: Physics-Aware Humanoid Whole-Body VLA via Multi-Brain Latent Flow Matching and Robust Tracking | Shanghai Jiao Tong University | Proposes a multi-“brain” VLA architecture for humanoid whole-body control that couples semantic intent, motion generation, and physics-aware tracking via latent flow matching. Demonstrates vision-language-guided coordinated upper/lower-limb control on a Unitree G1 humanoid for sequences such as walking, sitting, circling, and arm motions. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05410 |
| UltraDexGrasp: Learning Universal Dexterous Grasping for Bimanual Robots with Synthetic Data | Shanghai AI Lab et al. | Introduces a pipeline for large-scale synthetic dexterous grasp data (UltraDexGrasp-20M) using optimization-based grasp synthesis and planning-based demonstrations across multiple grasp strategies (pinch, tripod, whole-hand, bimanual). Trains a point-cloud-based policy with unidirectional attention that zero-shots to real bimanual grasping with ~81% success on novel objects. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05312 |
| PTLD: Sim-to-Real Privileged Tactile Latent Distillation for Dexterous Manipulation | Carnegie Mellon University; UC Berkeley; Meta (FAIR) | Trains privileged-sensor RL policies for dexterous in-hand tasks in simulation, then deploys them in real setups to collect tactile trajectories and distills a tactile state estimator (“tactile latent”). This enables tactile policies without tactile simulation; yields >2× improvement over proprioception-only baselines on in-hand rotation and 57% more goals in in-hand reorientation. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04531 |
| Risk-Aware Reinforcement Learning for Mobile Manipulation | University of Oxford & partners | Uses distributional RL (DPPO) with distortion risk measures to learn risk-sensitive whole-body mobile manipulation policies, then distills them to depth-based visuomotor students. Policies adjust risk at runtime and show improved worst-case performance in reactive, unmapped environments, balancing base and arm motions under depth sensing. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04579 |
| LEGS-POMDP: Language and Gesture-Guided Object Search in Partially Observable Environments | Brown University | Builds a POMDP framework for mobile object search that jointly maintains beliefs over object identity and location, fusing language, gesture, and visual observations. Demonstrates 89% success in simulation and real-world deployment on a quadruped mobile manipulator, relevant for language/gesture-guided mobile manipulation and navigation. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04705 |
