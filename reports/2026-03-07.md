| cuRoboV2: Dynamics-Aware Motion Generation with Depth-Fused Distance Fields for High-DoF Robots | NVIDIA | Presents cuRoboV2, a unified GPU-native motion generation stack for manipulators and humanoids that tightly couples planning, perception, and dynamics. Uses B-spline trajectory optimization enforcing smoothness and torque limits, and a TSDF/ESDF pipeline that builds dense signed distance fields over the full workspace up to 10× faster and 8× more memory-efficient than prior work while achieving ~99% collision recall. | PTLD: Sim-to-real Privileged Tactile Latent Distillation for Dexterous Manipulation | Facebook / Meta AI & collaborators (includes J. This estimator is distilled into the control loop, enabling the combined system to exploit tactile cues learned in reality while using sim-trained policies. Efficient implementations leverage the manifold geometry for real-time use. Demonstrations on S², as well as manipulator and mobile manipulator tasks, show improved tracking accuracy and path adherence versus prior manifold DS methods while enabling online adaptation of pose and stiffness trajectories. | https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05268 |
