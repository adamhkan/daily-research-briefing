## Executive Summary

- Strong emphasis on long-horizon and multi-stage manipulation: multiple VLA- and diffusion-based frameworks (AtomicVLA, AtomVLA, TempoFit, HSC-VLA, SaiVLA-0) target scalable skill libraries, temporal memory, and clutter-robust execution.
- Dexterous and bimanual manipulation advances span symmetry-aware learning, tactile-force control, and complex deformable object handling (EquiBim, TacDexGrasp, multifingered force-aware control, DexKnot, RoTri-Diff, DAISS, MoDE-VLA).
- Mobile and loco-manipulation see new planning and control abstractions, from graph-guided long-horizon indoor MoMA and low-cost teleop to distributed MPC and hierarchical humanoid loco-manipulation (MoMaStage, Low-Cost Teleoperation, ACLM, MetaWorld-X).
- Sensing and affordance work focuses on tactile-vision fusion and pose-aware affordances for robust contact-rich tasks and grasping in clutter (PhaForce, CONTACT, FlowTouch, Exp-Force, Viewpoint-Agnostic Grasp, RoboPCA, AffordGrasp).
- Data, fine-tuning, and deployment pipelines for manipulation policies mature via structured data engines, executability-aware demonstrations, MoE routing, PEFT improvements, edge-cloud and quantization strategies (Seed2Scale, FeasibleCap, LAR-MoE, RoboRouter, LoRA-SP, RAPID, DyQ-VLA).
- Surgical and medical manipulation platforms push toward autonomy with safety guarantees and dual-arm coordination under imaging feedback (SSP, Open-Source Laparoscopic Platform, DAISS).
