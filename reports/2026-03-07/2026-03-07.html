Executive Summary

  • Several works push long-horizon, memory-intensive manipulation forward via new diffusion, memory, and hierarchical policy designs (SeedPolicy, VPWEM, HD-ExpIt, Critic in the Loop, RoboMME).
  • Motion planning advances span GPU-accelerated dynamics-aware planning (cuRoboV2), multi-goal joint-space RRT* (Many-RRT*), graph-aware neural samplers (GAIDE), and manifold-based dynamical systems (CDSM).
  • Whole-body and mobile manipulation see new risk-aware visuomotor RL, physics-aware VLA control, and omnidirectional 3D perception for large-workspace humanoid manipulation.
  • Dexterous and bimanual manipulation are strengthened via sim-to-real tactile latent distillation (PTLD) and a 20M-frame synthetic dataset plus policy for universal bimanual dexterous grasping (UltraDexGrasp).
  • VLAs and LLM-based agents are probed and leveraged for interpretability, online control, and code-based policy evolution, including feature-level control, neuro-symbolic skill expansion, and in-context code rewriting.
  • Digital-twin, uncertainty-aware, and contact-tolerant methods address robustness: from Gaussian-splatting twins and risk-aware or distributionally robust planners to shared autonomy and adversarial robustness of visuomotor policies.

cuRoboV2: Dynamics-Aware Motion Generation with Depth-Fused Distance Fields for High-DoF RobotscuRoboV2 unifies B-spline trajectory optimization, GPU-native dense TSDF/ESDF mapping, and scalable whole-body dynamics on GPUs to generate smooth, torque-feasible, collision-free motions for high-DoF arms and humanoids. It achieves order-of-magnitude speedups and very high success rates in IK, retargeting, and locomotion tracking, showing a single stack can scale from manipulators to full humanoids.Link
Many-RRT*: Robust Joint-Space Trajectory Planning for Serial ManipulatorsMany-RRT* extends RRT*-Connect by planning simultaneously toward multiple IK solutions, alleviating the multi-arm-bandit problem caused by non-unique joint-space goals. Parallel goal trees yield dramatically higher success rates and lower-cost joint-space trajectories without runtime penalties, especially in cluttered workspaces.Link
GAIDE: Graph-based Attention Masking for Spatial- and Embodiment-aware Motion PlanningGAIDE is a neural informed sampler that encodes both scene spatial structure and robot embodiment as a graph, feeding this into a transformer with attention masking to guide sampling-based planners. By aligning sampling with geometry and kinematics, it improves planning efficiency and success rates over uniform, heuristic, and prior learned samplers.Link
Curve-Induced Dynamical Systems on Riemannian Manifolds and Lie GroupsThe CDSM framework builds stable dynamical systems directly on manifolds/Lie groups (e.g., SE(3), SPD) by combining tangential motion along a nominal curve with normal attraction toward it. Experiments show better accuracy and path adherence than existing methods, with demonstrations on manipulators and a mobile manipulator for geometry-aware motion generation.Link
Risk-Aware Reinforcement Learning for Mobile ManipulationThis work learns risk-aware visuomotor policies for mobile manipulators using distributional RL with distortion risk metrics, enabling runtime-adjustable risk sensitivity. Risk-aware teacher policies are distilled into depth-conditioned students that execute reactive whole-body motions in unmapped environments with improved worst-case performance.Link
PhysiFlow: Physics-Aware Humanoid Whole-Body VLA via Multi-Brain Latent Flow Matching and Robust TrackingPhysiFlow proposes a physics-aware, multi-brain VLA architecture for humanoid robots that fuses semantic motion intent with whole-body dynamic control. Through latent flow matching and robust tracking, it achieves stable, vision-language-guided full-body coordination on dynamic, limb-coordinated tasks.Link
Omni-Manip: Beyond-FOV Large-Workspace Humanoid Manipulation with Omnidirectional 3D PerceptionOmni-Manip uses a LiDAR-driven 360° point-cloud representation with time-aware attention pooling to power a visuomotor policy for large-workspace humanoid manipulation. The omnidirectional 3D perception removes the need for frequent base motions and yields strong performance in cluttered and beyond-FOV scenarios compared to RGB-D baselines.Link
UltraDexGrasp: Learning Universal Dexterous Grasping for Bimanual Robots with Synthetic DataUltraDexGrasp builds a synthetic pipeline that combines optimization-based grasp synthesis and planning-based demonstrations to create a 20M-frame multi-strategy dataset for bimanual dexterous grasping. A point-cloud policy trained solely on this data transfers zero-shot to real hardware, achieving over 80% success on diverse, novel objects.Link
PTLD: Sim-to-real Privileged Tactile Latent Distillation for Dexterous ManipulationPTLD sidesteps tactile simulation by training dexterous manipulation policies in sim with proprioception, then using real-world privileged sensing to distill a tactile state estimator that runs solely on tactile input. Incorporating this estimator significantly boosts sim-to-real performance on in-hand rotation and reorientation, outperforming proprioception-only baselines.Link
Iterative On-Policy Refinement of Hierarchical Diffusion Policies for Language-Conditioned ManipulationHD-ExpIt closes the loop between a high-level diffusion planner and low-level controller for language-conditioned manipulation via iterative environment interaction.
