## Executive Summary

- Dexterous and long-horizon manipulation sees multiple advances: tactile sim-to-real distillation (PTLD), massive synthetic bimanual grasp datasets (UltraDexGrasp), and several memory- and diffusion-based visuomotor policy architectures.
- Motion planning progresses on several fronts: GPU-accelerated dynamics-aware planning for high-DoF robots (cuRoboV2), multi-goal RRT* in joint space (Many-RRT*), graph-structured neural informed sampling (GAIDE), and risk/uncertainty-aware planners for both manipulators and mobile robots.
- Whole-body and humanoid control is extended with physics-aware VLA frameworks (PhysiFlow), risk-aware mobile manipulation policies, and LiDAR-driven 360° visuomotor control for large-workspace humanoid manipulation (Omni-Manip).
- Vision-language(-action) models permeate navigation and manipulation, enabling contact-tolerant planning with VLM reasoning, training-free frontier-based VL navigation, tri-system LLM/VLA control, and interpretable/controllable VLA feature interventions.
- Several works tackle robustness and uncertainty: evidential waypoint prediction (ELLIPSE), distributionally robust collision avoidance (U-OBCA), adversarial analysis of visuomotor policies, uncertainty-aware shared autonomy (SPIRIT), and CT-enabled contact-aware surgical planning.
- Tooling and infrastructure for motion/manipulation advance via digital twins (GaussTwin, textile-sorting twin), OMPL Python bindings with LLM assistance, and benchmarks/datasets focused on memory and hyperbolic multiview pretraining for manipulation.

| Risk-Aware Reinforcement Learning for Mobile Manipulation | Proposes a distributional RL framework to learn visuomotor policies for mobile manipulation with tunable risk sensitivity. | Many-RRT*: Robust Joint-Space Trajectory Planning for Serial Manipulators | Extends RRT*-Connect to simultaneously plan to multiple IK-derived joint-space goals, mitigating the risk of committing to a poor configuration for a given end-effector pose. | PhysiFlow: Physics-Aware Humanoid Whole-Body VLA via Multi-Brain Latent Flow Matching and Robust Tracking | Proposes a multi-brain VLA architecture for humanoids that couples semantic intent with physics-aware whole-body control via latent flow matching.
